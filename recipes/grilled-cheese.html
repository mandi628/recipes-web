Perfect Grilled Cheese

Description

This simple creation is a staple of the American diet. Variations can easily be found across the Internet, but our favorite version here, served with cream of tomato soup, frequents our family menu. The recipe is for one sandwich, but can easily be multiplied for a family dinner.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of your favorite bread
  • 1 oz. of your favorite cheese, sliced or shredded
  • your favorite mayonnaise
  • butter, room temperature

Steps

  1. Prepare your bread by spreading mayonnaise on one side of each slice.
  2. Heat a small skillet on medium-low to medium heat.
  3. Add butter to melt.
  4. Place first slice of bread, mayonnaise side down, in the skillet.
  5. Place cheese on the bread, spread evenly. NOTE: I prefer slices, becuase I don't like losing shredded cheese in the skillet.
  6. Top cheese with the second slice of bread, mayonnaise side up.
  7. Let it cook, uncovered, for about 5 minutes, or until golden brown. If it is browning too fast, lower the heat.
  8. Flip the sandwich, browning on the second side.
  9. Remove from pan, cut corner to corner, and serve.
