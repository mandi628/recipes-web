Rice

Description

Rice is a nice, basic thing to know how to make. You can use it as filling, make rice pudding, or a great savory side dish. This is something you should know how to make.

Ingredients

  • 1 part rice
  • 2 parts liquid

Steps

  1. Stir together the rice and liquid, and bring it to a boil.
  2. Remove from the heat, cover, and let it sit for 15-20 minutes, or until all the liquid is absorbed.

Variations

Parts can be 1/4 cup, 1/2 cup, 1 cup, etc. General, 1/4 cup of rice per person is recommened.

  • For plain rice, use water.
  • For savory flavored rice, use broth (beef, chicken, or vegetable).
  • To boost the flavor, add a pat of butter and some herbs when you combine the rice and liquid.
  • Use leftover plain rice to make Grandma's Rice Pudding.

Generally, you want to use water-based liquids to cook the rice. You can make a Hot Rice Cereal by cooking the rice in milk, with a handful of raisins or other dried fruits. Sweeten with sugar, maple syrup, or honey. NOTE: You would not boil the milk for this one. Keep the heat at medium.
