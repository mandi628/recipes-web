Homemade Mayoannaise

Ingredients

  • 1-1/4 C vegetable oil
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 C apple cider vinegar

Steps

  1. In a blender, mix 1/2 C oil, salt, and egg.
  2. With the blender on, slowly pour another 1/4 C oil, in a very thing stream, into the whirling mixture. Keep the blender mixing.
  3. Slowly pour into the whirling mixture half of the vinegar in a slow, steady stream. Keep the blender mixing.
  4. Slowly pour into the mixture another 1/4 C of oil followed by the rest of the vinegar. Continue blending.
  5. Slowly add the rest of the oil. You should now have a thick, white, creamy mayonnaise.
  6. Pour your mayonnaise into a clean container and store in the refrigerator. You can use this recipe in any recipe that calls for mayonnaise.

Cox, Janice. Natural Beauty at Home: More than 200 Easy-to-Use Recipes for Body, Bath, and Hair. 1st ed, H. Holt, 1994. pg 86.
